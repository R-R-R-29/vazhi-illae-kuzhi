### Project Description
[*Vazhi Illae Kuzhi* is a quirky and smart pothole detection app built using *OpenCV* and *Streamlit*. Just upload a road photo, and the app detects potholes by analyzing their shape and size.

What makes it stand out? Humor. Instead of just pointing out potholes, it roasts them with hilarious, sarcastic comments‚Äîturning frustration into fun.

And for a final touch, it creates a personalized *‚ÄúPothole Certificate‚Äù* highlighting the detected pothole, ready to download or share. It's a fun way to raise awareness about bad roads while having a laugh.

Perfect for civic-minded users, tech lovers, or anyone tired of bumpy rides, Vazhi Illae Kuzhi blends technology and local flavor for a smart, entertaining road-safety tool.]
